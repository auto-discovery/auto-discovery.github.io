One-line summary

The diagnosis. TTT-Discover commits to one root lineage early (H1), its exploration term self-disables (H3), and policies do local exploration around a frozen center (H4). Methods built from this diagnosis: rep-novelty wins AC1, QD-archive wins erdos by 10⁻⁶.
The caveat. The diagnostic ran at 64 rollouts/step; at paper-spec 512 rollouts (Phase A, in flight), the bare baseline reaches 0.380965 on erdos — better than every method we tested at 64 rollouts. H1-H4 mechanisms are real per-step; their practical cost may shrink at proper batch.
